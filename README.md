Data analysis for our Heimdall student Campaign at EISCAT Ramfjordmoen, Norway on 02.February 2026. 

This folder includes results from the Campaign (Eiscat measurements, UHF and VHF radar) and measurements from other instruments or satellites in the same time period and area. 
The goal of the analysis is to answer the question why we see such high electron enhancements in the F-region at night in the Radar measuerments.
